Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 10, 2026

1. Zero Data Collection Promise

PiParent operates under a strict Zero-Data architecture. We do not collect, transmit, store, or monetize any of your personal information, browsing history, network query logs, or device usage metrics.

2. Local Orchestration Only

All operations conducted by the application occur exclusively between the device running the application and your own self-hosted Pi-hole instance via your local intranet. No data passes through any 3rd-party intermediary servers controlled by us.

3. Third Party Services

To facilitate in-app subscription verification, PiParent utilizes RevenueCat and **Apple App Store** standard payment services. These services are only provided access to transactional identifiers for subscription activation and do not possess access to any metrics or family network configuration generated within the app.

4. Local Security & Keychain

Any credentials required to interface with your Pi-hole instance (such as password tokens) are stored exclusively on your local device using Apple’s native, hardware-encrypted iOS Keychain framework.
